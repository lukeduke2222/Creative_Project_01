Welcome to My Chicken Sandwich Rankings
If you haven't been living under a rock, you may have noticed that it has become trendy for fast-food places to release their own "unique" chicken sandwich. It has become my mission to try each of these sandwiches to determine the superior sandwich.
I will be ranking these sandwiches on three things. The most important rank will be on taste. I will also be taking into consideration price and
Rankings: Worst to Best
McDonald's: 3/5
Taste: 3/5
Price: Cheap
Consistency: 2.5/5
This sandwich is actually quite good for its price. However the consistency of the sandwich is not reliable at all which solidifies its low position.
Canes: 3/5
Taste: 3/5
Price: Higher
Consistency: 3/5
Made with tenders insead of a patty but the toppings and bun make this sandwich mediocere. With a high price, it is not worth it
Sonic: 4/5
Taste: 3.5/5
Price: Fairly Cheap
Consistency: 4/5
Better than expected and is very consistent in quality. Still a cheap taste but a good bargain.
Wendy's: 4/5
Taste: 4/5
Price: Fairly Cheap
Consistency: 4/5
The consistency is very good and the taste is superior in fast food, but you can tell the quality is that of a cheap sandwich.
KFC: 4/5
Taste: 4/5
Price: Higher
Consistency: 4/5
Very similar to Popeyes and comes down to the chicken used, in which Popeyes wins.
Chick-Fila: 4.5/5
Taste: 4/5
Price: Higher
Consistency: 5/5
A basic but very high quality sandwich with a very good taste. The consistency between visits makes this sandwich rank high.
Popeyes: 5/5
Taste: 5/5
Price: Higher
Consistency: 3/5
Extreamly good expecially with its pickels and sauce. You can tell it uses real chicken and its quality varies little by visit. These factors make this sandwich king.
